ZAIGARWARRIORS

Collect, Feed, Play, Earn, Trade

Zaigar Warriors, collection of 10,000 unique algorithmically generated NFTs collectible on the Binance Smart Chain that used for collectors to battle each other. Make them battle and earn! Warriors have four attributes which help them in battle: Attack Power, Defence Power, Speed and Health.

Battle Rules

1 You must have at least one Zaigar Warriors NFT to create or accept battle. Each warrior has a genome, aka "attack power", "defense power", "speed" and "health". When you mint your NFT, your initial "health" stat is "10", decreasing by one each time you lose a battle. Other stats are determined by your attributes and rarity. To participate in the battle, your "health" stat must be greater than zero. If your health stat is zero, you should feed your warriors with potions to increase health stat. The other way is to mint new NFT.

2 The outcome of Zaigar Warrior battling is depend on the warrior genome. Those who create the battle should be the "Attacker", those who accept the battle should be the "Defender". If your attack power and speed are higher than your opponent's defense power and speed, you win the battle. While the losing opponent's health stats and one of the other stats randomly decreasing, attack or defense power and speed of winner increases. Remember, neither side will know the strength of the other while battling.

3 The system involves two players, who wager ZFAI token on the outcome of an NFT battle. To join the battle, you must be on the same level as the NFT that created the war. If his level is 2, your level should be 2. Otherwise the system won't let you battle. Once entered the game, players will need to choose a warrior to play for the round. Battle creator and acceptor puts 2,000 ZFAI token as wager. The smart contract decides winner of the battle. Winner of the battle need to claim the wager x 2.

4 Your warrior evolves too! Our level system resolves on points, every warriors have to earn 200 exp point on each level, to get a new level, for that there's some ways: Winnings earn 5 points, but loses decrease in 2 points, so try not lose battles in order to evolve faster. Potions other than the health only potions increase point based on tier:

Caxambu and Everklena > + 3 points
Manegro and Amortentia > + 4 points.
In the fight rules its only permited that warriors should fight with warriors of same level.

Each ZAIGAR WARRIOR costs 0.1 BNB + Gas.

Own one of 10,000 unique Zaigar Warriors (ZW). Each of these NFTs has attributes that make them unique according to a defined rarity system. You can mint as many ZW as you want but once the 10,000 Zaigar Warriors are sold, it will be too late to get one at mint cost. However, dont worry, you will able to buy and sell at NFT marketplaces.
