ZAIGAR WARRIORS

Collect, Feed, Play, Earn, Trade

Mint-2-Play Zaigar Warriors, collection of 10,000 unique algorithmically generated warrior NFTs, is a game centered around cryptographically unique collectible warriors on the Binance Smart Chain that used for collectors to battle each other. Make them battle and earn! Warriors have four attributes which help them in battle: Attack Power, Defence Power, Speed and Health. The award for winning a battle is a ZFAI token and improved attributes.

Feed-2-Win As you win battles, NFT's attribute powers increase. But there is another way for you. You can feed your Zaigar Warrios and this helps you win battles. Feeding increases health, attack and defence power of the NFT warrior. There are 6 types of potions; Vitamix, Polyjuice, Caxambu, Everklena, Manegro and Amortentia. Strengthen Warriors means winning more battle, earning more ZFAI token and selling your NFT at higher price at marketplaces.

Play-2-Earn This is a system that involves two players, who wager ZFAI token on the outcome of an NFT fight. Once entered the game, players will need to choose a warrior to play for the round. Battle creator and acceptor puts 1,000 ZFAI token as wager. The smart contract decides winner of the battle. Winner of the battle need to claim the wager x 2. Moreover, the powers of the warriors winner has are improved to win the further battles.

Each ZAIGAR WARRIOR costs 0.03BNB + Gas.

Each warrior is unique, some are more rare then others. Weapons, skin color, clothes & stats - everything will be specific to your warrior. Mint for having one warrior, collect them and play to earn.
